How I Budgeted My First Part-Time Job Paycheck

Published: July 2025

Getting my first paycheck was exciting. But once the money hit my account, I realized I had no idea what to actually do with it.

I started by breaking it into three categories: spending, saving, and giving. I kept 60% for things I needed or wanted, saved 30%, and donated 10% to a local cause I care about. This simple rule helped me stay disciplined without overthinking everything.

If you're getting paid for the first time, don't just let it sit there. Make a plan, even if it's super simple. Your future self will thank you.
